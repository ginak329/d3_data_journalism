Correlation Discovered Between Age and Percentage of Smokers

As we analyze the latest information from the U.S. Census Bureau and the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System, we observed the relationship between the age and percentage of smokers. There seems to be a higher percentage of smokers between the ages of 36-40 across the U.S. West Virginia and Kentucky have the highest percentage of smokers, while Utah has the lowest percentage of smokers.
